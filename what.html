What is the E-Testing system ?

Welcome to the E-Testing system !


By using this website, you will be able to create tests and share it by simplest way.

Indeed, the "create a test" button will redirect you to the creation page.

On this page you can add questions, choose the question type and add as much propositions as you want.

But that's not all, you also will be able to add media on your questions. You can paste your Youtube videos and your image's links.


Be carefull, links must be on this form :

"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u_9svXpZhx4" or "http://www.universityfairs.com/files/directory_images/alytus-pic10.jpg"


After that, you have to enter your mail and your name. Then you can add as much mails as you want.

We will send your test to the persons you have choosen and you will receive their answers automatically on your mail box

Don't worry ! Each of your tests are protected and everyone will receive a personnal and unique token to access it.


We hope that you will enjoy our E-Testing system.
