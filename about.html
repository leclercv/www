About us

This project was created as part of an intership at Alytaus Kolegija in Lithuania.
We are two students from Lille1 University in France and we created this e-Testing System during our internship.

Thanks to our e-Testing System you will be able to create electronic-tests and we will send it automatically to the persons of your choice, then you will receice their answer by e-mail.

Our e-tests are protected by a unique token which is randomly generated, also only you and the persons you have choosen can access to your e-test.

We hope that you will enjoy our project.
